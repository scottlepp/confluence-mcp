# The Simpsons — Season Highlights

A quick look at the **best episodes** and memorable moments from Springfield's favorite family.

## Top Episodes

### Homer Goes to Space

- **Season**: 5, Episode 15
- **Plot**: Homer is selected by NASA as an average American to fly on the Space Shuttle.
- **Best quote**: *"In America, first you get the sugar, then you get the power, then you get the women."*

### Monorail!

- **Season**: 4, Episode 12
- **Plot**: A fast-talking salesman convinces Springfield to build a monorail.
- **Best quote**: *"I call the big one Bitey."*

### Cape Feare

- **Season**: 5, Episode 2
- **Plot**: Sideshow Bob is released from prison and stalks Bart.
- **Best quote**: *"No one who speaks German could be an evil man."*
